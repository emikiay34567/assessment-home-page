ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Erica Smith and I have been travelling 'round our lovely planet ever since I was 27 years old, when I decided to quit my boring nine-to-fiveoffice job. I decided that since I only live once I should dedicate this life to what I love to do most: travelling.

Since then, I have visited eighteen countries across four continents. But this is nowhere near the end. My goal is to visit all the seven continents and at least one-hundred countries. If you want to stick along with my journey then you can follow this vlog, as well as my socials to get more frequent updates.
