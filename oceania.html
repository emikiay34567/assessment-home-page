NEW ZEALAND

Do you want a sunny Christmas holiday? Yes, you heard me right. If so, then don't hesitate to book you Christmas present ahead of time by making it a trip with the destination of New Zealand.

A little bit of Geography: due to that New Zealand is located in the Southern Hemisphere the seasons there are the opposite than those in the Northern Hemisphere. Therefore, when it's winter in North America and Europe, it's summer in countries like New Zealand and in the continents Australia and South America.

Mountains and the Milford Sound

The Milford Sound is a fiord that is renowned by it's huge Mitre Peak as well as many rainforests and rainfalls.

The Sky-High Sky Tower

The Sky Tower is a whopping 328m tall and it's a major part of the Auckland Skyline, offering a majestic view of the city below.

A Natural Sauna: Wai-O-Tapu

The Wai-O-Tapu is a geothermal park that has a vivid scenery alongside that features a range of activities such as trails and picnic spots.
